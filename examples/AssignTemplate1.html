Leaf Collection and Cataloging

Description of Problem:

You are to go into the field and collect samples of various types of leaves and catalog them. You should draw a map of your collection area and mark where each sample was found on it.

Required Components:

You must collect at least one sample of leaves with each of the following characteristics:
  • palmate venation
  • pinnate venation
  • parallel margins
  • non-parallel margins

What to turn in:

You must turn in the following items:
  • Your collection of samples, mounted on a cork board, with each sample clearly labeled.
  • A report containing your analysis of each leaf, including identification and genera of the leaf.
  • Your map of the collection area, showing the location of each sample.
